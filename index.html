Who we are

Two millennia of wisdom,
in your hands

Ibi Pax — Latin for "Here is Peace" — is a new kind of Catholic publisher. We believe the two-thousand-year treasure of the Church was never meant to stay locked in archives and libraries. It belongs in the hands of every believer, every seeker, every soul searching for meaning.

Our app brings together the wisdom of the saints, ancient prayers, sacred art, spiritual readings, and contemplative music in a single, beautifully crafted experience — designed for daily use and quiet discovery.

"The beauty of the Church's tradition is not a relic.
It is alive."
What you will find

The Christian Heritage, Unlocked.

Ibi Pax brings the depth of Catholic tradition into one peaceful, distraction-free space. Experience each feature as it appears on your phone.

01
Your Daily Sanctuary

A peaceful home for your faith

From the moment you open Ibi Pax, you enter a space designed for stillness and beauty. The home screen gathers every treasure of the Catholic tradition in one elegant, distraction-free experience.

  • Daily saint, readings, and meditations at a glance
  • Beautifully crafted dark interface for focused contemplation
  • One-tap access to every feature
02
Lives of the Saints

Saints' Stories & Calendar

Each day, meet one of the great witnesses of the faith. Discover their story, their virtues, and the legacy they left for the Church — told with depth and reverence.

  • A new saint featured every day of the liturgical year
  • Rich biographies written with historical care
  • Searchable calendar of feasts and memorials
03
Contemplation

Guided Meditation

Rooted in Scripture and the spiritual masters — from the Spiritual Exercises to the writings of the mystics. Find stillness, every day.

  • Meditations drawn from Ignatian, Carmelite, and Benedictine traditions
  • Ambient sacred music for deeper focus
  • Timed sessions that fit your schedule
04
Roman Tradition

Ancient Prayers

Pray as the Church has prayed for centuries. A curated collection of prayers from the Roman tradition, with translations and spiritual context.

  • Latin text with side-by-side English translations
  • Historical and spiritual commentary for each prayer
  • From the earliest Christian invocations to the great hymns
05
Sacred Sound

Sacred Music

Gregorian chant and sacred polyphony to accompany your prayer. Curated playlists that quiet the noise of daily life and elevate the spirit.

  • Gregorian chant, polyphony, and organ masterworks
  • Playlists curated for prayer, study, and rest
  • Stream directly within the app
06
Visual Contemplation

Christian Art Weekly

Contemplate the masterworks of Christian civilization with guided reflections on faith and beauty. A new masterpiece each week, with rich commentary.

  • Curated paintings, mosaics, and sculptures from two millennia
  • Guided reflections on symbolism, theology, and artistic technique
  • High-resolution images for prayerful contemplation
07
Catholic Classics

Spiritual Readings

Access a growing library of Catholic spiritual classics — from the Church Fathers to medieval mystics — carefully selected and made readable for the modern soul.

  • Excerpts from Augustine, Aquinas, Teresa of Avila, and more
  • New readings added regularly to the library
  • Comfortable reading experience designed for reflection
08
AI Companion

Celestial Guide

An AI-powered spiritual companion, grounded in Catholic teaching, to help you navigate questions of faith, prayer, and the interior life.

  • Answers rooted in Scripture, Catechism, and Church Fathers
  • Conversational guidance for spiritual questions
  • Always faithful to the Magisterium

Our commitment
10%

of our net revenue, given back

We believe that beauty, faith, and heritage are worth protecting.
That's why Ibi Pax pledges 10% of its net revenue to organizations working to restore and preserve Christian patrimony — the churches, chapels, sacred art, and holy spaces that have shaped civilization for two thousand years.

These places are more than stone and paint. They are the memory of communities, the work of generations, and a living testament to what human hands can build when inspired by something greater than themselves. Too many are crumbling in silence. Every download helps keep them standing.

"To preserve what is beautiful is to honour those who built it — and those who will inherit it."
